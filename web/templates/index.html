World-Cup Simulator

What is this?

This is a probabilistic simulator for the upcoming football world-cup. It uses different rules to rate the strength of the teams and to calculate a team's chance to win in a match.
Repeating the simulation of the tournament plenty of times results in a probability distribution, that shows how likely a team is to win the finals.

What to do?


Go to New Tournament page
Click on Play World Cup below or in the navigation above.
Select Rules
Leave the rule weights at the default values or change them to your liking.
The rule weights decide how much your simulation will be influenced by the different rating methods.
Start the tournament
Then you can start the tournament and see what odds the different teams have to be world champion.

Play World Cup
